Services

Landscape Maintenance Services

Irrigation Repair

Prompt diagnosis and repair of drip systems, spray heads, valves, and smart controllers to prevent plant loss and water waste.

Edging

Crisp, defined edges along walkways, curbs, and planting beds that give your property a polished, professional appearance.

Mowing

Regular, precise mowing at the correct height for your turf type to promote healthy growth and consistent appearance.

Weed Control

Pre-emergent and post-emergent weed control programs tailored to Arizona's unique climate and plant communities.

Tree Removal

Safe, professional removal of dead, diseased, or hazardous trees with full cleanup and stump grinding available.

Overseeding

Seasonal overseeding with rye grass to maintain lush, green turf through Arizona's cooler months.

Fertilization

Customized fertilization programs that promote strong root development and vibrant color throughout the year.

Pruning

Expert pruning of trees, shrubs, and ornamental plants to maintain shape, health, and clearance from structures.

Seasonal Flowers

Vibrant seasonal color rotations in planting beds and containers to keep your property looking fresh every time of year.
